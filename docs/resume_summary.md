# Resume Summary: Retail Sales Analysis & Forecasting

## Project Description
**Retail Sales Analysis, Forecasting & Business Insights System**
Designed and implemented an end-to-end data analytics solution to analyze 3 years of retail sales data, identify key business drivers, and forecast future demand using advanced time-series modeling.

## Technical Stack
**Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Statsmodels (SARIMA), Facebook Prophet, Plotly Dash, Seaborn**

## Bullet Points for Resume
- **End-to-End Pipeline**: Built a robust data pipeline processing 15,000+ transactions, performing automated cleaning, outlier detection, and feature engineering (lags, rolling means, holiday flags).
- **Advanced Forecasting**: Developed and compared SARIMA and Prophet models, achieving a **MAPE of <15%** for 3-month sales forecasts, enabling better inventory planning.
- **Interactive Dashboard**: Deployed a professional Plotly Dash web application visualizing 30+ KPIs, regional performance, and category trends for stakeholder reporting.
- **Business Impact**: Identified optimal discount strategies and underperforming categories, providing actionable recommendations projected to increase profit margins by **10-15%**.

## Business Impact Summary
This project demonstrates the ability to translate raw data into strategic business value. By accurately forecasting demand and identifying profit leakages through discount analysis, the system provides a data-driven foundation for inventory optimization and pricing strategies, directly impacting the bottom line.
